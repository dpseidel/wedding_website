Honeymoon in Australia 2022

It’s long been a dream of ours to travel to Australia for the Australian Open, hosted every January in Melbourne. The dream was pushed back in 2020 and of course a no-go this year during the pandemic, but it’s naturally our dream honeymoon for when we can all travel again. We look forward to making a real trip out of it: exploring Melbourne and the tournament grounds during early play, escaping to wine country and hopefully a stay at the Lake House in Daylesford, a 4-star hotel, restuarant & spa that Dana can’t stop oggling. Additionally if time allows, we’d love to travel to Sydney to explore another corner of the world and possibly Cairns, to catch a glimpse of the Great Barrier Reef before it’s too late. We would be ever so grateful for anyone who wants to help us make this dream honeymoon a reality!

Our Dream Home

While we love our little apartment in Alameda, we are diligently saving our pennies to be able to afford our own home. Dana looks forward to a proper guest room and more space for her sewing, Owen longs for a garage and a private yard. We both look forward to having space add to our family with a pet or two. The goal is to be able to house hunt in earnest this spring and purchase a house of our own by summer or early fall 2021. We would be so honored by anyone who chose to help us with this endeavor as their wedding gift to us.

Dana’s Dream Sewing Machine

Dana finally has a hobby! After commissioning a quilt from a friend in the quilting business last fall, Dana began quilting on her own and has really fallen in love! In 2020, she finished 9 projects including 2 throw sized quilts she sent out for long arming. She currently sews on a BrotherCS6000i, a popular beginner machine that has been great for her as she learned to quilt this year. However now that she is a more advanced quilter and looking to be able to quilt larger projects on her own, she is dreaming about getting a more serious machine with a larger workspace and suite of features to help her hone her craft. Unfortunately, these models will cost upwards of 1500 dollars and with other large purchases on the horizon (house, wedding, …), this dream has been put on the backburner for now. If you’d like to support Dana’s craft, she would be delighted and forever grateful!
